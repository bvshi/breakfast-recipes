Perfect scrambled eggs

Here is the recipe for perfect scrambled eggs - soft, moist and delicious

Recipe by: me :)

Ingredients:

  • 2 large eggs
  • 20g of cold butter
  • 1 clove of garlic
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions:

  1. Put a knob of butter on the pan and turn on the stove on medium heat
  2. In a small bowl whisk the eggs and add salt and pepper
  3. After the butter is melted on the pan, put your eggs along with the garlic
  4. Using a spatula keep whisking the eggs - it will keep the eggs moist and create a perfect texture. When you see that the eggs are starting to stick to the pan, take the pan off the heat and keep whisking
  5. After a minute put the pan back on the stove and keep repeating this process. You can add some more butter in the meantime
  6. When you see that the eggs created a texture of your liking, you can stop whisking and you are ready to serve. You can garnish with more salt and pepper. Voila!
